# Code Book

This code book is a reference to understand the different features present in tidy.txt.

## Identifiers

* `subject`  - Unique identifier for the subject of the observations
* `activity` - Type of activity performed during the measurement

## Activity Labels

There was an aliasing process where integer values were renamed with an activity to make the dataset easier to understand

* `WALKING` (value `1`): During this measuremente the subject was walking 
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`): During this measuremente the subject was walking upstairs
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): During this measuremente the subject was walking downstais
* `SITTING` (value `4`): During this measuremente the subject was sitting
* `STANDING` (value `5`): During this measurmente the subject was standing
* `LAYING` (value `6`): During this measurmente the subject was laying
